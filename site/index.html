Welcome to

The Keepsake Company

For over a decade, The Keepsake Company has been a trusted name in celebration of life services throughout the Columbus, Georgia community and the greater Chattahoochee Valley region.

We understand that losing a loved one is one of life's most difficult experiences. That is why our dedicated team is committed to providing compassionate, personalized services that honor the unique life and legacy of every individual we serve. From traditional funeral services to modern celebrations of life, we work closely with each family to create meaningful tributes that reflect the spirit of their loved one.

In addition to our full-service funeral planning, we are proud to offer a curated collection of handcrafted memorial keepsakes, including personalized jewelry, custom urns, and remembrance items that allow you to carry the memory of your loved one close to your heart.
