Who We Are

Our Story

A Legacy of Compassion

The Keepsake Company was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to help families honor the lives of their loved ones through meaningful celebrations, personalized services, and lasting memorial keepsakes.

What began as a small family endeavor in Columbus, Georgia has grown into a trusted name throughout the Chattahoochee Valley. Our founder recognized a need for funeral services that went beyond tradition — services that truly celebrated the unique journey of each individual life.

Today, we continue that legacy by combining time-honored traditions with contemporary approaches to memorialization. Whether through a traditional homegoing service, a vibrant celebration of life, or a handcrafted piece of memorial jewelry, we ensure that every tribute is as unique as the person it honors.

Rooted in Community

As a locally owned and operated business, we take great pride in our deep connections to the Columbus community. We have been privileged to serve families from all walks of life, and we approach each family's needs with the same level of care and dedication.

Our involvement extends beyond our chapel doors. We actively participate in community outreach programs, support local organizations, and partner with churches and civic groups throughout the region. We believe that service to others is not just our profession — it is our calling.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, personalized celebration of life services and memorial keepsakes that honor the unique legacy of every individual. We are dedicated to supporting families through their most difficult moments with dignity, grace, and unwavering care.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in celebration of life services in the Chattahoochee Valley, recognized for our innovative approach to memorialization, our commitment to excellence, and our deep roots in the community we serve.

Compassion

We approach every family with empathy and understanding, recognizing the deeply personal nature of loss and remembrance.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in every aspect of our service, from planning to execution to follow-up care.

Community

We are committed to strengthening the bonds within our community through service, partnership, and outreach.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty, ensuring families can trust us during their most vulnerable moments.

Personalization

We believe every life is unique, and every tribute should reflect the individual personality and legacy of the person being honored.

Growth

We continuously evolve our services and approach to meet the changing needs of the families and communities we serve.
